Calle Crisologo Heritage

Located in the heart of Vigan City, Calle Crisologo offers a glimpse into the rich history of the Spanish colonial era in the Philippines. This iconic cobblestone street is lined with well-preserved ancestral homes made from adobe, brick, and hardwood, showcasing timeless craftsmanship. During the day, its historic architecture glows under the sunlight, while at night, the warm light of traditional lamps transforms the street into a romantic haven. Visitors can stroll through its charming pathways, enjoy a ride in a traditional kalesa (horse-drawn carriage), or explore local shops filled with handwoven textiles, antiques, and regional delicacies. Calle Crisologo is a testament to the enduring beauty of Filipino heritage and an essential destination for history enthusiasts and travelers seeking a unique cultural experience.

Bowden SunMaze at Red Deer

Located just south of Red Deer, Alberta, Bowden SunMaze is a vibrant oasis featuring thousands of golden sunflowers in full bloom. Visitors can wander through winding sunflower trails, take stunning photos, and experience the serene beauty of this family-run farm. The SunMaze is a seasonal attraction, best visited in late summer to early fall when the flowers reach their peak. Beyond sunflowers, the farm offers fresh produce, pumpkin patches, and scenic picnic areas, making it a perfect getaway for nature lovers and photographers alike.

Chesterman Beach

Located near Tofino on the west coast of Vancouver Island, British Columbia, Chesterman Beach is a breathtaking destination known for its expansive sandy shores and dynamic Pacific views. This serene location is perfect for surfing, long strolls, or simply soaking in the beauty of the ocean. It is a favorite for both locals and visitors seeking tranquility or adventure. The beach’s iconic sunsets and tide pools teeming with marine life make it an essential stop for nature enthusiasts exploring the rugged beauty of Canada’s west coast.
